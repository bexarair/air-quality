Bexar Air Mission

Bexar Air aims to create awareness of how our local air quality impacts the health of children within our community by creating a visual representation of the current air quality in Bexar County and comparing that information with city hospital data for pediatric asthma hospitalizations.

  • The Air Quality Index is based on measurement of particulate matter (PM2.5 and PM10), Ozone (O3), Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2), Sulfur Dioxide (SO2) and Carbon Monoxide (CO) emissions.
  • Receive text message alerts when a locations falls at or below an unhealthy level
  • Receive a daily text message at 5PM for tomorrow's AQI
  • Create custom locations to track the Air Quality of those locations
